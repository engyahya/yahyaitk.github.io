Method Overview

The workflow of the algorithm is depicted in Figure 1, showcasing the exploration of different scenarios involving the use of image satellites. Three scenarios were examined: 3 channels: Band 11 of Sentinel S2, NDVI (Normalized Difference Vegetation Index), and NDWIG (Normalized Difference Water Index)). 11 channels: All 11 bands of Sentinel S2. 13 channels: All 11 bands of Sentinel S2, along with NDVI and NDWIG indices. The segmentation task aims to accurately identify and delineate various water-related land cover types, such as open water surfaces, different degrees of soil waterlogging, vegetation affected by water, and atmospheric elements like clouds and shadows, utilizing imagery data captured by the Sentinel-2 satellite.
